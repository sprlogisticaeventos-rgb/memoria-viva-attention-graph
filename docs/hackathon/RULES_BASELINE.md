## Authority hierarchy

1. `official_rules`
2. `official_faq`
3. `official_update`
4. `written_clarification`
5. `internal_interpretation`

Higher-priority evidence overrides lower-priority material. Conflicts remain in
the changelog; records are superseded rather than deleted.

`official_schedule` and `official_openai_documentation` are supplemental
official evidence types. Official Rules determine the eligibility obligation
and remain controlling over the Schedule. OpenAI's current API documentation
supplies the dynamic country/territory list incorporated by the Rules.

## Evidence semantics

- **FACT** is directly supported by the named official source.
- **INTERPRETATION** is the repository's operational reading of that fact. It
  is not an official rule.
- **CONTROL** is the evidence or action used to demonstrate compliance. A
  control is not proof until it exists and receives human review.

Rule-record status values are `EVIDENCED`, `SUPERSEDED`, `DISPUTED`, and
`UNVERIFIED`. `EVIDENCED` describes source capture only.
